Operational Strategy Guide

  • Continuous Capture: The application monitors sound signals sequentially. Breaks or quiet pauses of any size will not lose your session state, thanks to the integrated self-healing reconnect engine.
  • Input Freedom: Type, overwrite, or adjust the live transcript feed container instantly with your keyboard; the Structured prompt panel recreates the final prompt template on every keypress.
  • Secure Sandbox: Microphone data is processed entirely client-side using browser-supported Web Speech APIs. No audio streams are recorded, logs tracked, or remote telemetry dispatched.
